Surrender a Bird

If you can no longer care for your parrot, we're here to help. We understand that circumstances change.

❤️ No Judgment

We understand that life circumstances change. We're here to help your bird, not to judge you. Your bird's welfare is our priority.

🏠 Safe Placement

We'll find the best possible home for your bird through our rigorous adoption process. We ensure every placement is permanent.

💙 Compassionate Care

Your bird will receive professional care, rehabilitation if needed, and love while waiting for their forever home.
