Emergency Avian Care

⚠️ Signs of Emergency

  • • Difficulty breathing
  • • Severe bleeding
  • • Loss of consciousness
  • • Severe trauma
  • • Inability to stand
  • • Extreme lethargy

📞 What to Do

  • • Call the emergency vet immediately
  • • Keep your bird warm and quiet
  • • Minimize handling and stress
  • • Transport safely in a carrier
  • • Bring any relevant medical records
  • • Have your bird's age and weight ready
