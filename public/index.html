Our Mission

Our name says it all. Sherry, Jerry, and our volunteers put their whole heart and soul into the Parrot rescue. We want you to feel part of our family and ensure every adoption is a forever match.

Dedicated Care

All of our volunteers have at least 5 years of rescue experience. We are here to help people that need to relinquish their birds with compassion.

Education First

We want to educate the public so they are aware of the commitment. Proper care and understanding are key to a happy parrot life.

Forever Homes

We strive for every adoption to be permanent. Our rigorous process ensures that birds find safe, loving, and lasting environments.

Want to join our flock?

We are an all-volunteer rescue. We are foster-based, meaning all our parrots live in private homes. We are always looking for dedicated volunteers and fosters to help us save more lives.
